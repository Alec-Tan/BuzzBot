BuzzBot can use two prefixes: "!buzz" and "!b" 
**!buzz help** - Sends this help message 
**!buzz emote X** - Use to search for and send X emote from FrankerFaceZ. Example: "!buzz emote YEP" 
**!buzz dog** - Sends an image of a random dog 
**!buzz dog_breed X** - Sends a random image of a dog of breed X. Example: "!buzz dog shiba" 
**!buzz cat** - Sends an image of a random cat 
**!buzz cat_breed X** - Sends a random image of a cat of breed X. Example: "!buzz cat bengal"
